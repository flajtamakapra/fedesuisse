Members and friends of the Swiss community,

The Federation of Swiss Societies in Eastern Canada presents its new website. It is now accessible on computer, tablet and cell.

On the tab « National Day Program » you have a link to our program of the SWISS NATIONAL DAY 2018 and to the schedule of the day, August 4th at Mont Sutton. As well as some photos of this event organized by the Federation of Swiss Societies in Eastern Canada (FSSEC / Fédé). We will eventually add a link to programs and photos from previous years.

Do you know the Swiss clubs in Eastern Canada? How to contact them? Their activities?

Simply click on the "Swiss Clubs" tab and you will find a list of all active Swiss clubs and societies in Eastern Canada. By clicking on the logo of a particular club, you will have their mission and contact information.

In the "Events" tab, you will find the monthly calendar of the activities of the Swiss clubs of Eastern Canada, members or not of the Federation. To view a current or upcoming event that interests you, click on the name to see details and an invitation if applicable. These activities are open to club members and also non-members for the majority of them. You should consult this calendar regularly.


We invite you to visit this site to eventually find interesting links.
